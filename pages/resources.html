Free Resources for Kids

Bible-based videos, activities, and devotionals to help children grow in their walk with the Lord.

For Kids & Families

Videos, Activities & Devotionals

Good News TV

Could you and your kids use some Good News in your lives right now? Join us each week and hear the best news ever! You'll hear about the amazing things God did in the Bible as well as the amazing things He can do for you. Children can watch these videos each week to help them grow spiritually.

Watch on YouTube

UNITE Channel

God's Word shared in a fun, engaging way to draw children to understand and apply biblical truths. Verse songs are dynamic ways for children to learn and memorize God's Word. Each Bible lesson clearly teaches the Gospel. Children can watch anytime -- over breakfast, after school, in the evening!

Watch UNITE

Wonder-Fun Activity Book

Looking for a fun children's activity? The Wonder-Fun activity book is filled with coloring pages, puzzles, mazes, word scrambles, drawing activities, and much more! This action-packed Bible activity book shares the Good News of Jesus Christ and helps children understand the truths of God's Word.

Download Free

Stuck at Home Devotionals

This set of 30 devotionals is designed to help kids deal with challenges they may be facing. Each devotional has been carefully selected to help children recognize that God is still in control and to give them practical help in relying on Him.
