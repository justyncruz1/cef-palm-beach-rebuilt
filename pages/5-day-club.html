5-Day Club

Five days of fun, friendship, and the Gospel—right in your neighborhood.

Summer Outreach

What Is 5-Day Club?

5-Day Club is an exciting, fun-filled hour and a half held each day for five consecutive days. Children experience dynamic Bible lessons, creative learning activities, inspiring missionary stories, meaningful songs, and life-changing Scripture memorization.

It’s completely free and open to all boys and girls ages 5–12, regardless of religious background. Parents are welcome to attend with their child.

The Process

How It Works

1

A Local Church Partners With Us

Churches co-sponsor 5-Day Clubs to reach children in their community. There is no cost for the church or children.

2

Trained Students Lead the Way

Young people ages 12–18 trained through our CYIA program lead each club with energy, care, and biblical truth.

3

Children Hear the Gospel

Over five days, children hear the Good News, learn Scripture, make friends, and are encouraged to attend a local church.

Our Teachers

Who Teaches 5-Day Club?

5-Day Club teachers are young people (ages 12–18) who have received specialized training through our Christian Youth in Action program. They are carefully screened according to CEF’s child protection policy and are passionate about sharing God’s love with children.
