Serve With Us

There are many ways to use your gifts to reach children with the Gospel. Start your journey today.

“As each has received a gift, use it to serve one another, as good stewards of God’s varied grace.”

— 1 Peter 4:10

How to Get Started

Three Simple Steps

1

Sign Up

Complete our Volunteer Interest Form so we can learn a little about you and where you want to serve. A member of our team will contact you within 1–3 days.

2

Screening & Approval

Complete and clear the background screening procedures. The safety of every child is our highest priority.

3

Training & Equipping

Attend locally-based training that will prepare you to reach children effectively. You’ll receive everything you need to get started.

Opportunities

Ways to Serve

Teach Good News Club

Lead weekly after-school Bible clubs in elementary schools. Training and all materials provided.

Support a Club Team

Help with setup, games, small groups, and encouragement. No teaching experience required.

Lead a 5-Day Club

Students ages 12–18 can serve through CYIA summer missions. Adults can host or support a club.

Ready?

Take the First Step

Fill out our quick interest form and a team member will be in touch to help you find the right fit.
