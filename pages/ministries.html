Ministries That Reach Children

From after-school clubs to summer outreach to youth missions training, every program is designed to share the Gospel with boys and girls across Palm Beach and Martin Counties.

Our Programs

Three Ministries, One Mission

Every program is built on the same foundation: sharing the Gospel of Jesus Christ with children through engaging, age-appropriate Bible teaching.

Good News Club

Good News Club is an after-school ministry where trained volunteers meet with children in public elementary schools. Each week, kids experience exciting Bible lessons, songs, Scripture memory, missions stories, and review games—all in a safe, fun environment with parental permission.

  • Meets weekly during the school year
  • 19+ schools in Palm Beach & Martin Counties
  • Free for all children
Learn More

5-Day Club

5-Day Club is an exciting summer outreach held for 90 minutes each day over five consecutive days. Children experience dynamic Bible lessons, creative learning activities, inspiring missionary stories, meaningful songs, and life-changing Scripture memorization.

  • 5 consecutive days each summer
  • Taught by trained CYIA students
  • Free and open to ages 5–12
Learn More

Christian Youth in Action

CYIA is a short-term local missions experience for students ages 12–22. After a week of intensive training, students return home to teach 5-Day Clubs and share the Gospel with children in their own communities.

  • Ages 12–22
  • One week intensive training
  • Summer missions in your community
