NextGen

Invest monthly in the next generation. Your recurring gift fuels everything we do.

Monthly Partnership

Why Monthly Giving Matters

When you become a NextGen partner, you don’t just make a one-time gift—you become part of the team. Your monthly support provides the consistent funding we need to plan ahead, expand into new schools, train new volunteers, and reach more children with the Gospel.

$25/month

Provides Bible lesson materials for one Good News Club for a month

$50/month

Helps train and equip a new volunteer to lead children’s ministry

$75/month

Supports a CYIA student’s week of missions training

Any Amount

Every dollar goes directly to reaching children in Palm Beach County

Become a NextGen Partner

Your monthly gift makes a lasting difference in the lives of children across Palm Beach County.
