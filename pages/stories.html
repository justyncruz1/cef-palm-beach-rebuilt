Impact Stories

Real stories of God's faithfulness in the lives of children and families across Palm Beach County.

Featured Story

Jacqueline's Story

Jacqueline's journey with CEF began as a young girl attending Good News Club. The seeds of the Gospel planted in her heart during those after-school meetings grew into a deep faith that would shape her entire life. Today, Jacqueline serves as a Ministry Coordinator, pouring into the next generation of children the same love and truth that was shared with her.

Testimonials

More Stories of God at Work

A Mother's Gratitude

"When my daughter started attending Good News Club at her elementary school, I wasn't sure what to expect. Within weeks, she was coming home singing worship songs and telling me Bible stories. The volunteers are so loving and dedicated. This ministry has made a real difference in our family."

From Student to Teacher

"I attended CYIA as a nervous 14-year-old who had never taught anyone anything. By the end of the training week, I was leading Bible lessons with confidence. That summer, I helped lead three 5-Day Clubs and saw children respond to the Gospel. It changed my understanding of what God can do through anyone who is willing."

A Volunteer's Heart

"I retired three years ago and was looking for a meaningful way to serve. A friend invited me to help with Good News Club, and I haven't looked back. Every Wednesday, I get to see children light up when they hear about God's love. There is nothing more fulfilling."

Every Story Starts with Someone Who Said Yes

Will you be next? Whether you serve on the front lines or support from behind the scenes, your yes makes a difference in the life of a child.
