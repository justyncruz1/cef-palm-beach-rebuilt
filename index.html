Reaching Children in Palm Beach County

Every Child Deserves to Hear the Good News

We partner with local churches to bring the Gospel to children through after-school clubs, summer programs, and youth discipleship across Palm Beach and Martin Counties.

Our Mission

Evangelize

Sharing the Gospel clearly with every child through Bible-centered programs in schools and communities.

Disciple

Nurturing young believers in their faith through consistent teaching, mentorship, and biblical community.

Establish

Connecting children and families to local churches where they can grow, serve, and belong.
